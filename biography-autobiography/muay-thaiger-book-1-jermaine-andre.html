What creates a World class combat fighting martial artist? What drives a man to step into the cage against other great men to fight with almost no rules until one can no longer stand? What occurs within one's life to hone a passive child into what most view as a fighting beast? MUAY THAIGER "Cub & Tiger" books are the youth & teen years of Jermaine Andre's life. Learn in this autobiography what it took to create the 5x U.S. Champ, 2x World Champ, UFC Vet, author and one of the world's most sought role models. From street fights and wars, armed stand offs, a year in solitary confinement, wild animal training, spiritual ventures with native American tribes, diversity in education, adopting the code of the samurai and more... you will learn that Jermaine Andre's devastating 15 year professional fight career was the least exciting time of his life.
