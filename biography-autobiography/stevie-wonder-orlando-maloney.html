This outstanding compendium is the harvest of a long term research and hundreds of exclusive interviews with a remarkable level of access to the very closest circles of Stevie Wonder's family - including himself. Cutting through tabloid gossips, Orlando traces the real story behind the Stevie we see and hear today, from his development as a child star through the blooming of his talent to his ever changing personal appearance and extraordinary publicity performances. This major biography incorporates the behind the scenes story to many of the landmarks in Stevie Wonder's life. Objective and revealing, it carries the endorsement of all of Orlando's bestsellers, exquisite research, brilliant storytelling and ultimate documentation.
