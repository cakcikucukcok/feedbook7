accepted to England's prestigious Leeds University ...to a position at the Jamaican Mission to the

United Nations  and a Carnegie Fellowship  that continued her journeys across the world...to marriage and life in Trinidad...to the challenge of facing widowhood with two young children and ultimately to happiness in a new marriage and life in the United States.
