A Book Review

This is a moving story that will make you laugh...and at times...will bring you to tears. It's a story about an unexpected inheritance that uproots Haley's life as she rediscovers who she is. This beautiful woman values family, cherishes real friendships, and is deeply blinded by color. It's a story that will give you faith that true love still exist. You will enjoy timeless music from a radio station that was once on its last leg. And even though life finally seemed wonderful, murder showed up and showed out in the worst way. See if you can guess whodunit!
