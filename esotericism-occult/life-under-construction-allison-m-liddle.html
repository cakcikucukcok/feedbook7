Have you ever felt stuck in a life that you didn’t choose? Then you need to begin designing your life to effect real, positive change!

 

Change is essential to self-growth, yet even when change is planned, we all go through times when we feel like there’s no rhyme or reason for what’s happening around us. Life Under Construction: Designing a Life You Love will help you transform change from a chaotic storm into an adventure… an adventure that you’ll wake up every day feeling excited about.

 

What changes are you facing in your life today? Maybe you’re starting a new career. Perhaps you’ve become a new parent. Maybe you’re transitioning into retirement. Or perhaps you’ve decided that enough is enough and you simply want to become a better version of yourself. Whatever your goal, the key to success is harnessing change by designing your life. Life Under Construction will help you do just that.

 

No matter what your own life’s construction story is, you will need help and support to navigate through change with purposeful intent. In her first book, Allison Liddle, a John Maxwell Team Certified keynote speaker, executive coach, and leadership trainer, will share with you her own life-under-construction story and offer simple, yet effective, techniques that you can use for designing your own life. She’ll help you uncover your true passions, teach you to think big and dream even bigger, share advice from leaders in their fields, and guide you as you transform into a better, more purposeful version of yourself. Your life is yours to design, why not make it a life you will love?
