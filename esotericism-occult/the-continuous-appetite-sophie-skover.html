Have you ever found yourself mindlessly shoving food into your mouth after a bad day? Life is supposed to feel wonderful, but when you are controlled by the fridge, pantry, or overpowering food cravings, your life can become depressing and dreadful to live.

The Continuous Appetite will show you how to stop running to food when life presents a challenge, by taking you on an interactive journey that helps you understand and heal the imbalance in your body, emotions, mind, and spirit. The result is having a harmonious relationship with food where youre able to eat to fuel your body, and taste the pleasure along the way.
