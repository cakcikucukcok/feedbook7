Would you like to live your best life exactly as you want it? Luminaries on the Loose was written for you, so you can identify where you are in your life. You'll be able to figure out what to do, and what's likely to be coming up next on your horizon. A valuable guidebook to take you by the hand along the Archetypal Trail, so you can: • Discover more about yourself • Achieve more direction, fulfillment and meaning, everyday • Live your greatest life - precisely as you would like your best life to be You'll track your own life's journey through the three ancient and time-tested phases and twenty-two essential steps in life's ultimate quest for success, health, happiness - and love. Follow the transformational steps and stories to increase your awareness of the possibilities open to you. Or just turn to any chapter for a good fun read! Enjoy the authentic voices of leading Australian Authors who share their real experiences, woven together in this compelling, motivating and inspirational book.
