Just Be Love begins with a moving account of a mystical experience David Schroeder had that inspired him to write this book. He explores the many aspects of love from both our human and spiritual perspectives. David reflects on love that is familiar to us, and he takes us to the hidden, mysterious, and paradoxical aspects of where love is often not realized. Just Be Love weaves Davids personal and professional experience, reflections, and insights on love, as well the teachings and wisdom of the spiritual masters and poets of many traditions. This book guides us to contemplate and explore again and again that our purpose and reason for being is to Just Be Love.
