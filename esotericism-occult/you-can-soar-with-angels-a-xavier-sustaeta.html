You Can Soar with Angels is a cheery testimonial of basic but often forgotten truths regarding our spirituality, and how we can reclaim it.

Beginning by how modern technology is constantly modifying our use of time and our expectations, the narrative proceeds to analyze the source of our current disenfranchisement with the spiritual, while offering practical guidelines for reverting to our heart as the main moral compass to be followed.

Angels, in a reformatted, contemporary presentation, are the main vehicle used by the author for drawing attention to the many transcendent possibilities open to all of us in our quest for self-improvement.

Peppered with quotes from influential thinkers, and illustrated with poignant short stories, the text includes two fun-practical Work Books (See an Angel/Become an Angel), as well as a full set of tongue-in-cheek instructions for successful Angel Sightings and an Angel Equation Formula for their clear identification.

It is only with the heart that one can see rightly; what is essential is invisible to the eye.
