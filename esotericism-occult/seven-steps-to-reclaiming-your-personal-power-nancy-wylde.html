A journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step. A journey of self-empowerment begins with seven. In Seven Steps to Reclaiming Your Personal Power, author Nancy Wylde introduces you to seven key elements required to tap into your personal power.

This concise guide seeks to help you manage your inner world and bring about things you desire in your outer world. Through a manifestation of personal power, you can learn to release aspects of your life that do not serve you and worries that hold you back. Through empowering, inspirational self-talk and thought energy, you can find ways to replace them with a compelling vision of the future that draws you towards it. Discover visualisation and meditation techniques that can change your life for the better, as well as affirmations of your personal power that are designed to enhance your personal development.

The techniques in Seven Steps to Reclaiming Your Personal Power are practical and easy to incorporate into your life. Make the decision, follow the steps, and begin reclaiming your personal power today.
