Charles Fillmore's last book, "Atom-Smashing Power of Mind", is compiled from a series of articles written by him over many years.

Charles Fillmore was ahead of many theologians and scientists of his day and ours in understanding the inescapable relationship of the spiritual and physical worlds. In "Atom-Smashing Power of Mind", Charles Fillmore approaches the spiritual realm from the viewpoint of science. He takes it apart, demystifies the "holiness" of our relationship to God and the universe and remystifies us by showing us Jesus' teachings as scientific theory.
