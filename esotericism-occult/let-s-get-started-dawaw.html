This exercise booklet guides the study of embracing Christian living on earth and attaining eternal life. After completing these self-paced exercises, you will have a better understanding of the Christian lifestyle / Christian walk.

Each lesson includes

1. a brief description of the topic, along with its purpose and functions

2. key scripture passages that support the topic and serve as a helpful resource for future reference

3. research questions designed to gain a better understanding of the topic and its application in everyday living

4. something to review to help begin processing the new knowledge and learning to apply it into relationships with others

5. space for journaling comments and/or questions.

The bonus section gives a snapshot that helps to simplify the understanding of the book of Revelation.
