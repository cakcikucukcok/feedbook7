We go through life each day living a life that appears 'normal' to us. We make choices and decisions that are seen as either 'right or wrong.' We sometimes don't understand the bigger picture in life. This book takes you on a journey of self-discovery, to establish a better understanding to why we might be acting in a certain way or doing the things that we do. We all have the ability to change things, but how can we if we don't understand why or how. The author looks at different scenarios and experiences that could be happening in our everyday lives. Most of the things in this book, each and every one of us has either experienced or might be going through, which we can all relate too.
