“I Am Free to Be the Best of Me!”

 

This book is the third one of the trilogy” Personality Development Geology, the first two being” Living

Intelligence or the Art of Becoming! and “Soul-Refining.” Together with the book “I Am Free to be the Best of Me!” Dr. Ray tries to create THE MANUEL OF LIFE and SELF – CREATION.

 

All three books are meant to help young people become more life-aware and better focused on self-creation and self-installation in life. A shift in thinking should be instilled in their minds – from having to Being and Becoming! As a great Russian psychologist, Leo Vygotsky, writes “Psychology exists for one purpose only – to shape you into a personality!”

 

Dr. Ray believes that life without a goal of self-realization is pointless. With the help of the auto-suggestively installed AUTO-MEDIA, a sharpened perception of the digital reality, you will have a chance to enhance your immense creativity, expand your “spiritualized intelligence”, and develop high self-consciousness.

 

We are here to discover ourselves!

 

Naturally, our mission in life is to realize the gifts that we are granted from the Above and raise the level of our spiritual vibrations to get connected to the Universal Intelligence that the present-day science proves to be digital. Dr. Ray presents the Know - How of self-creation in five levels: physical, emotional, mental, spiritual, and universal and unveils the holistic paradigm of a personality creation correspondingly as:

 

Body + Spirit + Mind + Self-Consciousness + Super-Consciousness- = Self-Creation

 

“When a person knows his mission on earth, he lives most purposefully and effectively. So, do not teach just the subject, teach the whole person!”(Leo Vygotsky)
