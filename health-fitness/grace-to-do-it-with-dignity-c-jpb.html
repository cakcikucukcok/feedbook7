Dignity I write this book to help those who are also going through trying times,struggling situations and devastating experiences. This book is intended to encourage, strengthen, enlighten and educate persons and people while patiently waiting for and moving toward great victory and victories. This book is not intended to hurt, harm or expose anyone or anything. For LOVE is EVERLASTING and LOVE is ETERNAL. And I am LOVE. Dignity is the quality or state of being worthy of esteem and or respect. This poem describes it best: "Dignity" Dignity is the ability to STAND STRONG and tall in the face of adversity While being able to bow to the elderly and *yet crawl with children Dignity is taking a stand for your BELIEFS Without closing your mind to another's opinions Dignity is being an example by your DEEDS and through your WORDS Avoiding gossip, anger and lies Dignity will manifest itself in the warmth of your SMILE, the depth of your LOVE and *your KINDNESS *to *and *for your FELLOWMAN Mychal Wynn *words from Jacqueline Brown
