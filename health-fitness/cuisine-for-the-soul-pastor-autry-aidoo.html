In this book you will find a little of this and a lot of that. That is the way that I M. I have written a few pieces of poetry, a sermon or two and some recipes. I hope you enjoy! In preparing a meal you always think about it and them gather the necessary ingredients that help make Arcady meal. Life is not any different. Many beginners, when cooking, need a good cookbook or recipe to follow. My sermons and recipes are to you, the reader, as a guideline when staring off in cooking or ministry. This poetry is to remind us of who we are: I am your prophetess, your diva, your cook, your housekeeper, your cleaner, your gleaner. Your body, your twine, Im your peace of mind And you are mine. Life is like an onion you take it with delight and afterwards you wonder whatever made you bite. look forward to the next book . . . My man doesnt know how to love me
