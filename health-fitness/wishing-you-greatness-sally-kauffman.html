Do you need to be reminded of your greatness? Do you want to move toward your dreams and desires of greatness? We all have a touch of greatness, and in Wishing You Greatness, author Sally Kauffman offers a series of affirmations to help remind ourselves, our families, our loved ones, our friends, our neighbors, and our world about our greatness. Inspired by her grandsons positive outlook, Kauffman shares an array of advice to move toward our dreams and for connecting with our greatness every day: Be an example of greatness. Be astounding with your greatness. Be humble with your greatness. Be in abundance with your greatness. Allow yourself to fulfill your greatness. Anticipate your greatness. Appreciate your greatness. Read it all, pick it up every day, or choose just one page. Wishing You Greatness presents a collection of positive quotes and ideas to illustrate how to connect with your greatness each and every day.
