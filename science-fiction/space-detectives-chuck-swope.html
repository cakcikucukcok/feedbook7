Space Detectives is 3 stories about Steve Titanic & Gordon Gatt, Intergalactic Investigators, Space Detectives if you will, working for Earth Force One to help keep the Universe free of criminals and safe for ordinary citizens. In the three stories the two detectives encounter a madman who has an Earthquake machine and wants to extort billions not to use it. As proof he devastates the planet of Veronda with it. Then Steve and his good friend Doctor Mogumber go on a time travel adventure only to have their time travel machine stolen by an alien race that plans to enslave the whole universe from the beginnings of time. And finally, Steve Titanic & Gordon Gatt encounter the same madman from the first adventure, this time he has hacked into the Pentagon's computers. Steve and Gordon track him down only to find he's getting help from a superior alien creature. Don't miss these thrilling stories.
