Paralyzed by the sickened state of the world, - the material excess, the human indifference and the Great Disconnect brought about by digital devices, a young man seeks refuge and comforting solace in the dreams and memories of his youth, of a simpler, more innocent time. Author Robert Miljan presents his first novella, in which he gives us a singular striking and daring indictment of the modern age of industry and technology, encoded within a web of stunning aesthetic prose and allegory, - a "sweet, sad song of where it all went asunder."
