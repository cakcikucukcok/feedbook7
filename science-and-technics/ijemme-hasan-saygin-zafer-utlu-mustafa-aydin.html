Engineering Faculty of Istanbul Ayd?n University has started to publish an international journal on Electronics, Mechanical and Mechatronics Engineering, denoted as “International Journal of Electronics, Mechanical and Mechatronics Engineering (IJEMME)”. We have especially selected the scientific areas which will cover future prospective engineering titles such as Robotics, Mechanics, Electronics, Telecommunications, Control systems, System Engineering, Biomedical, and renewable Energy Sources.

The manuscipt titled “PRODUCTION OF CLARITHROMYCIN LOADED CHITOSAN MICROSHERES BY SPRAY DRYING AN OPTIMIZATION STUDY ” by Duygu ALTIOK dated year 2014, Volume 4, Number 1; is withdrawn. 

We have selected only a few of the manuscripts to be published after a peer review process of many submitted studies. Editorial members aim to establish an international journal IJEMME, which will be welcomed by Engineering Index (EI) and Science Citation Index (SCI) in short period of time. 

Prof. Dr. Zafer UTLU
