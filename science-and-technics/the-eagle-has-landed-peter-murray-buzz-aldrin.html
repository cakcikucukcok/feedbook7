It’s important that we reflect upon and celebrate our experiences, to learn from them and to push for more small steps and giant leaps. “One Small Step” commemorates the 50th Anniversary of the Apollo 11 Moon Landing and all of those individuals who helped make it a success. It inspires us to keep dreaming and reaching while saluting the mission and those who made it happen. I hope you enjoy it as much as I do and that it will rouse you to take at least one small step of your own. The most significant event of our time and the start of hundreds of Space expeditions for mankind, we celebrate the 50th anniversary of Apollo 11's journey to the moon and back. With amazing images of the event supplied by NASA and an introduction from astronaut Buzz Aldrin we hope you’ll agree this book is an amazing resource.
