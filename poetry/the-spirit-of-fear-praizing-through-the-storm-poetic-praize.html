The air is calm. You see no signs of clouds. Pure tranquility. But in an instant, your tranquility can transform into the middle of a raging storm. It's dark. Dreary. Cold. Everything you've come to love in this world can become your worst nightmare. And then who do you run to? What are you afraid of? In a world that screams, "What are you made of?" If you said "nothing," that's a lie. I can see the fear in your eyes... Praizing Through The Storm is the second volume of The Spirit of Fear. It shows the fear and doubt that accompanies going through tribulations and the triumphs of trusting God through the storm.
