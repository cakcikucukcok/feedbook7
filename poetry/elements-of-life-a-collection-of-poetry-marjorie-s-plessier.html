The Elements of Life are Earth, Air, Fire, Water and Love.

Earth represents hope and happiness in our lives.

Air represents the randomness of life which is as unpredictable as particles blowing in the wind.

Fire represents the burning of anger or negativity in life.

Water represents the tears of sadness that we all shed.

The ultimate element of the five, Love, represents the passion and desire of our hearts.

The theme of this book was inspired by the authors favorite movie, The Fifth Element directed by Luc Besson.
