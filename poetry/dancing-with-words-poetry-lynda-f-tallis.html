Words from the Cauldron Born

My Journey with Poetry

The Cauldron of the Underworld:

In which inspiration, wisdom and knowledge are brewed.

Living with dyslexia.

As a child I had poetry and stories locked inside but as I have dyslexia it was hard to put them down onto paper. In the 50s dyslexia was not recognised and no support was available.

Now with Computers my words can take flight with all the new software available. I joined the Order of Bards, Ovates and Druids. And along the path of a Bard, I found the courage to show and publish my work and as an Ovate the spiritual voice now as a Druid the knowledge to share and encourage others to find there gifts and share them with all.

I hope my words inspire other dyslexic sufferers to unleash their hidden talents to find their written voice.

Poetry based on my spiritual path of Druidry, of nature, people and places that have inspired me over the years.
