Puzzled Isles of Spirit: My intentions, with these words, are to reach as many wandering souls, as possible, to achieve an energetic shift of consciousness, towards serious and mutually beneficial CHANGE. With the "hope" that the reader is able to tap into their "SOURCE", of their own existence - with an open mind and a hunger for knowledge of the Universal Puzzle pieces and forming a personal belief and value system, based on information that "Feels Good" Living... This guide is for people seeking more than what they have found, so far. The more you learn, the more questions arise AND more POTENTIAL towards a purposeful, soulful existence. These are simple tools for awakening our "divinity", to manifest our destiny and touch others with the thoughts and actions that strengthen us, as a whole, with our differences accepted - sharing the purity of TRUTH - OUR SHARED STRENGTHS! We begin with The isle of the Universe - Mother Earth; The isles of Movement and Time - Continental Alliance; Isles of History - Cultural Recordkeeping; Isles of Power - Balancing Power; Isles of Vision - "R"evolution; Isles of Consciousness- Unity of Consciousness; and, Isles of our Souls - Unity of Thought. I hope readers will digest what feels and tastes good, assimilates what nourishes their soul and eliminates what does not feel right to them, with and about these words.
