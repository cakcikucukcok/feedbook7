KATIE BLOOM'S FIVE-POINT PLAN TO HAPPILY-EVER-AFTER 

Foster a happy, healthy environment for my child-to-be. Even though my ex left us, I'm so excited to be a mom. 

Find a new career path, now that the Peach Leaf Historical Society is closing. 

Make this year's Pumpkin Festival the best Peach Leaf, Texas, has ever seensince it will be the last! 

Create the perfect family with the only man I've ever lovedmy childhood BFF, Ryan Ford. He's back in town for the first time since he married his pregnant high school girlfriendand our chemistry is as off-the-charts as ever. 

Recover from Ryan's gorgeous hazel stare and let him know how I feel, how I've always felt. He left home years ago, but I'm not letting him goever again!
