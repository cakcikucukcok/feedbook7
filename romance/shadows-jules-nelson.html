Take a trip back in history.. to a small farm in Michigan... and meet Emma Fern Wells.

Emma lives a peaceful life. Her days are filled with caring for her Da and two brothers. She has no time to socialize. At 18, most of her friends are already married. Emma has accepted that she has little chance of being courted with her heavy workload. And, even though, she feels content with caring for her loving family, Emma is lonely.

But the day a young man rescues Emma from a scoundrel in town, everything changes. Soon her life is filled with laughter, joy -- and worry. When Emmas family notices these changes in her, it brings forth troubles of their own.

As Emma struggles to follow Gods will, she comes to realize that God does not simply guide us by events along our path...

...sometimes you are guided by the shadows...
