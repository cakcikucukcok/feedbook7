"The Rainbow Rug, please take a little tug. Now look and see what colour could it be?" Young readers will revel in the fun of play-tugging at the rainbow rug tassels and guessing what colour comes next. A very enjoyable read-aloud book that appeals to both parent and child thanks to the catchy rhythm combined with charming illustrations. This colour-themed read is repetititive and playful, helping young children easily recognize and identify the rainbow colours, matching them to everyday objects. Whatsmore, the countdown of colours from seven to one reinforces early number learning. A must for every young child's book shelf!
