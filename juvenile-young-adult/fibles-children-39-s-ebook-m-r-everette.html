Fibles : Children’s eBook by author M.R. Everette comes with 12 stories and 36 illustrations. The stories of Fibles are about the untruths that may befall on the adorable characters, and the ensuing outcomes of their actions. In each story, the characters encounter real-life issues like an idle mind in “The Guppible One”. However big or small, the characters learn how to deal with them, and they end with a small lesson or moral attached to them. These short and entertaining stories are told through a cast of loveable animal characters with quirky names, and are uniquely tailored for today’s children learning life’s lessons.

Fibles has multiple illustrations per story with the initial illustration having the main character names embedded to help bring the stories to life for the adult and child alike. Have fun and read Fibles aloud!
