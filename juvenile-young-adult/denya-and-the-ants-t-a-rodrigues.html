Welcome to the Lilibene Village, home of the bean people. They share a border with the Redbelly ants, who are on the brink of civil war within their hive. Denya, a young bean with an adventurous spirit, is trying to find her place in the world. When she and her friend Paylo wander over the border of their territory, they find themselves dealing with the ants. A coup d'etat catalyzes a state of war between the ants and the bean. Having wandered into the Wilderness, Denya is unaware of these events. However, she may hold the key to saving both the Lilibene Village and the Redbelly Hive. But first, she must learn who she is.
