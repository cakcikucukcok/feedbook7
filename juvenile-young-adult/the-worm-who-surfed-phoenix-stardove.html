A timeless children's story about a little worm with a BIG dream. Illustrated with bold bright colors and easy to read, the author, Phoenix Stardove takes us on a journey of not giving up no matter your station in life. Slippy the worm dreams of surfing and tries to share his dreams with others. As he grows he encounters characters not exactly seeing his dream, and they offer only self-doubt. Slippy holds tightly to his dream. After getting rejected multiple times, and almost giving up, Slippy meets a friendly purple spider who offers empathy and support. Slippy renews his confidence and hope leading to the most important moment in his life. In the end, we are offered a beautiful moral in the form of a poem.

All of us, especially children need the positive affirmations to give them the support, love and hope they need to achieve their dreams. This story is for those of us that need help believing in our dreams no matter our station, talents, or situation. You can achieve your dream if its meant to be, it will be no matter what anyone says.
