Forced to live with her evil Aunt Rose, she is finally rescued by her father, a soldier who had been wounded in Afghanistan. They become best friends. When they chase a small animal in the tree, they find themselves in a strange world. They are in prehistoric Texasa beautiful world that is filled with danger.

The first in a series, this book contains a murder mystery, adventure, and a glimpse of Texas as it was millions of years ago.
