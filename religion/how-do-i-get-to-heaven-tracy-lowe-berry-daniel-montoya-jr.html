Travel with creative Carter inside his vivid imagination and help him discover how to get to Heaven. Explore numerous delightful journeys that will take him high into the sky in an effort to meet Jesus. Will he get to Heaven if he flies in a giant red rocket ship or in a 747 jumbo jet plane? After pondering several imaginative ideas that will take him up thousands of feet into the sky, he learns the only way to get to Heaven is to accept Jesus Christ as his Lord and Savior. Carter engages young readers. Adults will see Heaven through the eyes of a child. HOW DO I GET TO HEAVEN is uplifting, filled with rhythm and rhyme and is certain to perk childrens curiosity about God and Heaven.
