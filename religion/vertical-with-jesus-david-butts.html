Christians are people who have accepted Christ’s call to a vertical lifestyle. The Bible tells us to seek first Christ’s Kingdom and His righteousness before being concerned with the horizontal stuff of life and ministry around us. Vertical with Jesus is a 30-day devotional that encourages a balance between connecting with Jesus through prayer and doing the work of the Kingdom. Each day you will be stimulated in your connection with God, and you will consider how to keep focused on Him as you live the life He has called you to. An excellent resource for an individual or for an entire congregation to focus on for a 30-day period.
