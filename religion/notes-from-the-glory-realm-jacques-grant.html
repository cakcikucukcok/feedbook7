Psalm 91 begins by saying, They who dwell in the secret place of the most High, shall abide under the shadow of the Almighty. They, meaning us, who dwell (live, reside, stay) in the secret place. The secret placeGods most holy place, the Holy of Holiesshall live, play, remain, work, raise families, enjoy friends, and love one another under the shadow or covering or umbrella or become insulated and/or engulfed by the Almighty Father of creation himself! What a wonderful thought, to be able to be embraced and covered in this security and understanding and, in these boundaries, live a most blessed, prosperous, and favored life! But how? How do we remain? How do we dwell? How do we abide and rest in the glory realm of Gods graciousness and favor? The author of this book, through his own personal journey with God, attempts to help the reader enjoy the most wonderful existence and presence this world and this life have to offerthe secret place, the glory realm.
