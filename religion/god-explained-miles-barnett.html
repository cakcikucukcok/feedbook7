All religions state that their God is loving, kind and forgiving. Yet, most religions have radicals that state, If you dont believe in my God I am going to kill you.

This book shows how thirty religious groups and denominations within these groups view God. The understanding individuals will receive from reading this book will allow them to discuss religion honestly and openly with others.

Hopefully, through this knowledge, people will have a better understanding of how God is viewed by different religions.

This knowledge should allow individuals to perceive and understand when religious prejudice is being brought forth.
