The purpose of having a good life with God, Jesus, and the Holy Spirit is to better understand our situations on earth. Riches, glory, wealth, and Christian religion are not things of the past. Over time, I hope that reading this book will help you give glory to Jesus and know how to earnestly pray for your riches in heaven. A life of abundance is a promise. Seeking God in prayer is key. This book focuses on previous Christian experiences, Holy Bible verses, and faith in Jesus. We all need time to grow in our faith. Having faith in Jesus Christ is the way to heaven. The verses describe how to be a doer of the word by having faith, praying for understanding in wealth, blessing your riches, walking in holiness, prospering, and succeeding with Jesus Christ. The Holy Spirit has touched many spirits and revealed an incredible journey of faith to be on our way to heaven.
