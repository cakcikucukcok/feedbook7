Discover the amazingly creative things you can do with your glue gun!

You've used your glue gun to make some pretty nice stuff, but chances are you've never used it to its full potential. Bursting with over-the-top ideas, this full-color book teaches you the tricks, hacks and even 3D modeling techinques that take your projects to the next level. From holiday crafts using scented and glow-in-the-dark glue sticks to 3D objects constructed with hot glue, you’ll be amazed at how versatile a simple stick of glue can be.

Including step-by-step instructions for over 50 fun crafts, this book has something for everyone. Kids can enjoy using low-temperature glue guns to bring their creative ideas to life in the form of window clings and holiday decorations. Adults will find ways to entertain and decorate with Pinterest-perfect crafts like votive holders and debossed monograms that no one will guess came from the hot glue gun.
