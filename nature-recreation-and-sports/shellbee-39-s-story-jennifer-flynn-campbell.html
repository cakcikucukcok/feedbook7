If you’re reading this, you’re probably a human—that’s great, I consider all humans to be one of my littermates, for whom I strive to provide love, joy, comfort, laughter, and tireless protection. Every living organism has a story that leaves an external print on earth . . . mine just happen to be paw prints. I hope my stories fill your heart with love while washing away the difficulties of your day. Come, let’s take a walk together and create a memory . . . On our walk, I promise to look at the experience through your eyes while sharing mine. Then together we will create a feeling of comfort knowing we are not alone. Let’s go and be free!!
