A Nigerian dwarf goat is a breed of goat that is much smaller in size and weight as compared to the usual goats. Goats belong to the caprine family. The most important thing for anyone who is interested in miniature livestock to know is that these are not dwarfed versions of larger livestock. They are new breeds that just tend to look smaller in size and proportions.

Nigerian dwarf goats are known to be playful and adorable, and can be clever and timid at the same time. They are ideal for people who are looking for loyal and lovable pets. They are also used for meat and milk.

It is important that a prospective buyer learns all they can about the animal. If you wish to buy and domesticate a Nigerian dwarf goat, you should make an attempt to understand the unique mannerisms and antics of the animal. This will enable you to understand the pet better.
