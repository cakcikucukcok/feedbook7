The book helps to understand and see that even a pug can be a service dog. With the dedication from the dog, the owner, and the right trainer, any dog can be a service dog. We rescued our pug from a shelter. When people say we were her angels, we clearly say no, she was our angel. We were just lucky enough to see her.
