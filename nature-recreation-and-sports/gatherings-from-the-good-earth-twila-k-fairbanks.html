Connect to the Magical Spirit of Mother Earth

I AM a child of the earth.  I take off my shoes and feel her heartbeat through the soles of my feet in the dark green grass.  We are part of each other.

The opening words of this book invite the reader to settle in and join the author on a journey through the seasons and experiences of the natural world.  Combining personal reflections with lessons passed down through generations, Twila Fairbanks weaves a vision of a simpler, more connected life.

Walk the earth mindfully month by month with joy and gratitude.  This collection of essays will be a valued companion as you journey through the year, the seasons, months, and the days.  Learn to look at the world differently, finding peaceful places in nature and joy in each day.  Giving ourselves space and time to do this creates awareness in every aspect of life, and may be the most important thing we will ever do.
