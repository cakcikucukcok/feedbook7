Los Angeles prison.The first day as a penitentiary guard it's very difficult for Khyla. She  suddenly find herself in a reality full of dark sides that she didn't even imagined. Yet this sad and depressing place, gives her something really unexpected. An encounter of a few seconds is enough to change her life. Is it possible to fall in love at first sight with two eyes as grey as a foggy day and as cold as ice? This is what Khyla asks to herself, nevertheless she can't do anything to avoid the feeling that Matthew arouses in her, a young detainee rich with charm. Attracted in the vortex of sensuality that he proposes to her, she can't refuse to dive headfirst in a one sided-love, in a story destined to finish badly. And when Matthew asks her to help him to evade from jail, she agree without worrying too much about the consequences. So it begins a nail-biting travel among the street of California where pain and love, fear and hope mix in a story that leaves breathless.
