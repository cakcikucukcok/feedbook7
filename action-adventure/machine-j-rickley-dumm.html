There are many beautiful women but none like this. 

She is statuesque. Her eyes change colors from brown to green or blue, and always with a sparkle of diamonds.

What does she live for? What is her power? Above all, she seeks survival and adaptation. She’s naïve, somewhat childish, and inexperienced, but if threatened, she’s a warrior, fierce and exact.

She doesn’t think; rather, she processes, and totally lacks social skills and graces. She has no feelings or emotions, but she’s learning.

She seeks to right wrongs but not to harm. Being too timid does not leave a proper impression; being too aggressive defeats her commitment.

She is like you—innocent, vulnerable and destructible.

She is not like you. Jo Machine is uniquely uncommon and dissimilar to what you’ve ever experienced before.
