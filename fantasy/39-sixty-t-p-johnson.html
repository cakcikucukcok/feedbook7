T.P. Johnson brings us a thrilling tale of a tormented man who finds himself thrust into a search for his lost family. Aedan O’Beirne hates time. He hates it despite his inability to mold or shape the maddening increments people use to insert significance and organization into their otherwise meaningless lives. With a scant three days until Christmas, he discovers his wife and young son are missing. With no help other than his own resources, he enters a painful struggle against his adversary, time. He must find his lost family before it is too late, or he risks losing them forever.
