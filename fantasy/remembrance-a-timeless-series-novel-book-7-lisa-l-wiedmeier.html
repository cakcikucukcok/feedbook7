They say death is the final chapter, but for Cheyenne, it was the beginning of a new story.

Yet for Kitty and Hunter, their mother passed away in childbirth. Only knowing her through stories from their father, it comes as some surprise when they find her alive in Paris. Cheyenne, however, has no memory of her children, nor can she bring herself to leave the one who's held her captive all these years.

Colt, Kitty, and Hunter must, therefore, fight to bring Cheyenne back and restore her to her former self. But fate will not make their journey easy, and once again the clans must face rejuvenation or total destruction.
