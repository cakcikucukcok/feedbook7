A high-priced thief who can’t turn down a challenge. A cocky, young mage whose power has caught the attention of the local Mage-Lord. A royal scribe who stands on the brink of an imperial invasion.

This fantasy short story collection contains three stand-alone adventures by Jacquelyn Smith, author of the World of Lasniniar epic fantasy series: The God’s Eye, The Unclean, and Wayward Scribe.

The Unclean and The God’s Eye are back-to-back winners of the Writers’ Journal Annual Fiction Contest, and have both appeared in Writers’ Journal Magazine.
