What’s the first thing people do when they see a dragon at a coffee shop?

Take a selfie, of course.

Magic has been unleashed on Earth.  A third of the world’s population has changed.  Elves, Dwarves, Orcs, and Gnomes are no longer confined to fantasy stories.  Now, they’re your neighbors.

For David and Rose, a world of magic is nothing new.  Rose is a Dragoness, after all.  Even with the the old world turned upside-down, they have a good life together—until his daughter’s birth mother, Manya, calls.  The Change made Manya a humanoid tiger, and she could lose Aparna forever unless David asserts his parental rights.

David is a Hero, an experienced adventurer and demon-slayer with a magic sword and a Dragon on his side.  Taking care of a four year-old girl should be no problem, even if she does have wings and a way of getting what she wants.

Really, how hard could it be?
