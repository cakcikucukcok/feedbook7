In the afterlife, any righteous spirit can enjoy the bounty of Heaven, but only the elite have the option to serve the Kingdom of Heaven as angels. For those who choose to serve the Kingdom of Heaven, they have the honor of being protectors of Gods children on Earth. This introduction to the OMNI universe primarily focuses on the unique relationship between two angels named Donavus and Gadieon as they rise through the ranks of angels to become superior protectors. Gadieons zealous nature will eventually cause him to betray the code of Heaven and launch an all-out assault that could destroy the Earthly and Heavenly realms. Can the forces of Heaven unite in time to stop Gadieons plan? Who will answer the call in this great time of need? Come see the day in the life of a Servant of the Kingdom!
